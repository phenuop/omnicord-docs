Getting Started

Everything you need to know to begin your Omnicord journey

Step 1: Register

Before you can do anything, you need to register with Omnicord. This creates your profile and unlocks all features.

.register

You only need to register once! After that, all your progress is saved automatically.

Step 2: Get Your First Alien

Everyone starts with a Starter Box that contains your first alien.

  1. Use .open starter to open your Starter Box
  2. You'll receive a random alien from the starter pool
  3. Use .aliens to view your collection

Your first alien is free! Additional aliens can be obtained through fragments, boxes, and luckspin.

Step 3: Start Playing

With your first alien, you can now:

  • Battle other players with .battle
  • Level up your alien by fighting villains
  • Earn rewards from .vote and daily events
  • Join or create a guild with .createguild

Alien & Fragment System

Learn how to collect, unlock, and master your alien collection

How to Get New Aliens

To unlock new aliens, you must collect alien fragments through various methods. Each alien requires a specific number of fragments to unlock.

Method 1: Fighting Villains

A villain appears in your chat every 24 messages. When a villain spawns, fight it using the battle system. Upon defeating a villain, there is a random chance you'll receive a fragment for a specific alien.

Method 2: Luck Spin

Use the .luckspin command for a chance to win complete alien unlocks, alien fragments, and other rewards. This is one of the fastest ways to get rare aliens.

.luckspin

Method 3: Vote Boxes

Support the bot by using .vote. Each vote gives you a vote box that can contain alien fragments, complete unlocks, Tyden, and more.

Method 4: Trading

Trade aliens and fragments with other players using .trade.

Tracking Your Progress

Check Alien Details

Use .detail <alienName> to see how many fragments you need to unlock an alien.

.detail heatblast

Check Your Fragments

Use .frags to see how many fragments you have collected.

.frags

Example: Heatblast: 3/6 — You have 3 out of 6 fragments needed.

Example: Unlocking Heatblast

  1. Use .detail heatblast — requires 6 fragments
  2. Fight villains, use .luckspin, and open vote boxes
  3. Use .frags to check progress: "Heatblast: 3/6"
  4. Once you have all 6 fragments, Heatblast unlocks automatically!
